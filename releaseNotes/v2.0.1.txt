Hello everyone!

We're excited to announce that version 2.0.1 of the Domo Documentation Hub is now available! This release adds a brand-new networking guide and includes content cleanup across several articles.


🌟 What's New

🔐 New Guide: Set Up AWS PrivateLink with Domo
Customers looking to connect to Domo over a secure private network now have a dedicated, step-by-step guide. The new "Set Up AWS PrivateLink with Domo" article walks through the full configuration process, helping admins establish private connectivity without routing traffic over the public internet.
Thanks to John Nielsen for authoring the guide and Jared Peterson for adding it to the navigation.

📦 Domo Sandbox Article Streamlined
Duplicate content was removed from the Domo Sandbox article, making it cleaner and easier to navigate for users setting up or managing sandbox environments.
Thanks to Mamta Bolaki for the cleanup.

🧹 Repository Housekeeping
Six temporary migration utility scripts (used during the programmatic MDX conversion) have been removed from the repo now that they've served their purpose, keeping the codebase tidy.
Thanks to Jared Peterson for the cleanup.


🙏 Thank You

A heartfelt thank you to everyone who contributed to this release. Every addition and cleanup helps our readers find what they need, faster.

If you have questions or feedback, please reach out — we're always happy to help!
