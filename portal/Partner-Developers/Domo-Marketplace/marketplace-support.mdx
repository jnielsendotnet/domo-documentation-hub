Try us: Our technical support consultants are there 24/7 to provide world-class service and help you make the most of your Domo experience.

[support.domo.com](http://support.domo.com)

You can also check the status of your App approval or ask any questions on the App approval process by reaching us at: <a title="mailto:appsubmissions@domo.com" href="mailto:appsubmissions@domo.com">appsubmissions@domo.com</a>

## App Requirements

Please ensure that your app meets all the requirements in the [App Requirements documentation](/portal/Partner-Developers/Domo-Marketplace/marketplace-requirements) before submitting your app to the Domo Appstore. All Apps built in Domo follow these same procedures. Please be ready with an appropriate name, relevant [tags](/portal/Partner-Developers/Domo-Marketplace/marketplace-submission#supported-tags), description, icon and a screenshot for your app.
