[support.domo.com](http://support.domo.com)

You can also check the status of your App approval or ask any questions on the App approval process by reaching us at: [appsubmissions@domo.com](mailto:appsubmissions@domo.com)

## App Requirements

Please ensure that your app meets all the requirements in the [App Requirements documentation](/portal/c9db4daa8edfe-appstore-requirements) before submitting your app to the Domo Appstore. All Apps built in Domo follow these same procedures. Please be ready with an appropriate name, relevant [tags](/portal/d4f8eee792eb6-appstore-submission#supported-tags), description, icon and a screenshot for your app.
