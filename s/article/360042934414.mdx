---
title: "Adding Company Pages"
---


You can add selected card pages as company pages. This locks the order of the pages in the navigation bar as well as the order of cards and collections on the pages. You do this in **More >** **Admin > Company settings > Company page settings**. If a company page has subpages, the subpages are also locked. Other users who can access the pages cannot reorder company pages or add, remove, or reorder cards in the pages, or move copied or duplicated cards to a company page.

You can only add company pages if you have an "Admin" default security role or a custom role with "Manage All Company Settings" enabled. For more information about default security roles, see [Default Security Role Reference](/s/article/360043438953). For more information about custom roles, see [Managing Custom Roles](/s/article/360043438973 "Managing Custom Roles").

<Note>
  **Notes:**

  * You can also lock the content in a personal (non-company) page by clicking the
  <img src="/images/kb/ka0Vq00000010qn-00N5w00000Ri7BU-0EM5w000005vOFT.png" style={{width: 20, height: 20, display: 'inline', verticalAlign: 'start', margin: '0'}}/> icon in that page. You do *not* need to have an "Admin" security role to do this—any page owner can lock his or her own page. Locking a page in this way means other users cannot copy, move, remove, resize, or reposition cards in the page unless they have an "Admin" security role. For more information about locking content in a personal page, see [Lock and Unlock Dashboard Content](/s/article/360042923814 "Lock and Unlock Dashboard Content").
  * Company pages do
  *not* appear in **Manage Pages**.<br/>
   To manage a page, the page cannot be a company page. For example, to add subpages to a company page, you must remove the page as a company page, then add the subpages. For information about managing pages, see [Managing Pages](/s/article/360043428253 "Managing Pages").
  * You
  *cannot* hide company pages from your personal view.
  * You can resize cards in a company page.<br/>
   For more information about resizing, see [Changing the Size of Cards in a Page](/s/article/360042923594 "Changing the Size of Cards in a Page").<br/>

</Note>

**To add company pages,**

1. Click **More** **> Admin**.
     
   The Admin Settings appears.
2. Select **Company settings > Company page settings**.
3. Click **+** **Add page**, select the Pages you want to add as company pages, then click **Add**.
     
   Subpages are also set as company pages.

**To order company pages,**

1. Click **More** **> Admin**.
     
   The Admin Settings appears.
2. Select **Company settings > Company page settings**.
3. Under **Company Pages**, order the Pages by dragging the <img src="/images/kb/ka0Vq000000115J-00N5w00000Ri7BU-0EM5w000005vNsJ.png" style={{width: 20, height: 20, display: 'inline', verticalAlign: 'start', margin: '0'}}/> icon next to a page to the position you want.
4. (Optional) Order Subpages by expanding the Subpages of a Page, then dragging the
   <img src="/images/kb/ka0Vq000000115J-00N5w00000Ri7BU-0EM5w000005vNsJ.png" style={{width: 20, height: 20, display: 'inline', verticalAlign: 'start', margin: '0'}}/> icon next to a Subpage to the position you want.
     
   <Frame><img alt="" src="/images/kb/ka0Vq000000115J-00N5w00000Ri7BU-0EM5w000005vNsH.png" style={{width: 369, height: 140}}/></Frame>
**To remove a page as a company page,**

1. Click **More** **> Admin**.
     
   The Admin Settings appears.
2. Select **Company settings > Company page settings**.
3. Under **Company Pages**, click **Remove** for Pages you want to remove from your list of company pages.
