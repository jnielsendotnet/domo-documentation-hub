## Intro

Slido is an interactive web tool that lets participants/ audience engage through live polls, Q&As, quizzes, and word clouds during your meetings or conferences whether online or in-person. You can obtain the audience's feedback, insights, and analytics using Slido's intuitive and easy features. To learn more about the Slido API, see ([https://datapi-eu1.slido.com/redoc](https://datapi-eu1.slido.com/redoc "https://datapi-eu1.slido.com/redoc") ).

You can connect to your Slido account in the Data Center. This topic discusses the fields and menus that are specific to the Slido connector user interface. To add DataSets, set update schedules, and edit DataSet information, see [Adding a DataSet Using a Connector](https://domo-support.domo.com/s/article/360042926274?language=en_US "Adding a DataSet Using a Connector").

## Prerequisites

To connect to your Slido account and create a DataSet, you must have the Organization ID and you need to select the Region where your instance is located.

## Connecting to Your Slido Account

This section enumerates the options in the **Credentials** and **Details** panes in the Slido Connector page. The components of the other panes on this page, **Scheduling** and **Name & Describe Your DataSet**, are universal across most connector types and are discussed in greater length in [Adding a DataSet Using a Connector](https://domo-support.domo.com/s/article/360042926274?language=en_US "Adding a DataSet Using a Connector").
