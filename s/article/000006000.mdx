## Intro

Procore helps construction companies, property owners, project managers, contractors, and partners collaborate on large-scale projects. It provides shared access to documents, planning systems, and data. Use the Domo Procore connector to get lists of budget items, work orders, equipment, projects, vendors, and more. To learn more about the ProcoreV2 API, visit their page ([https://www.procore.com/platform/procore-connect.php](https://www.procore.com/platform/procore-connect.php)).

You can connect to your ProcoreV2 account in the Data Center. This topic discusses the fields and menus that are specific to the ProcoreV2 connector user interface. To add DataSets, set update schedules, and edit DataSet information, see [Adding a DataSet Using a Connector](/s/article/360042926274).

## Prerequisites

To connect to your ProcoreV2 account and create a DataSet, you need the email address and password associated with your ProcoreV2 Account.

## Connecting to Your Procore Account

This section enumerates the options in the **Credentials** and **Details** panes on the ProcoreV2 Connector page. The components of the other panes on this page, **Scheduling** and **Name & Describe Your DataSet**, are universal across most connector types and are discussed in great detail in [Adding a DataSet Using a Connector](/s/article/360042926274).

## Credentials Pane

The Domo ProcoreV2 connector uses OAuth for authentication, so you don’t need to enter credentials in Domo. Select **Add Account** to open the Procore OAuth screen. Then, enter your Procore email address and password, or choose an existing Procore account in Domo.

Once you have entered valid ProcoreV2 credentials, you can use the same account to create a new ProcoreV2 DataSet. You can manage connector accounts in the **Accounts** tab in the Data Center. For more information about this tab, see [Manage Connector Accounts](/s/article/360042926054).
