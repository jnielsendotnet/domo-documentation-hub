### Intro

This reference guide gives examples of the available functions that can be used in the Add Formula and Group By tiles in Magic ETL. For a complete list, refer to the **Functions** list inside the Magic ETL Formula Editor, as displayed in the image below.

<Frame><img alt="Add_Formula_Editor.png" src="/images/kb/ka0Vq0000000wbt-00N5w00000Ri7BU-0EM5w000005vOuw.png" style={{width: 679, height: 564}}/></Frame>
<Warning>
  **Important:** While the Formula Editor looks similar to the calculated field editor in Analyzer, they are not the same. You can copy any calculation you have made into the Formula Editor and it will work, but not all of the functions allowed in the Formula Editor will work inside of a calculated field. This is due to the increased processing power available to DataFlows versus Analyzer.

</Warning>
