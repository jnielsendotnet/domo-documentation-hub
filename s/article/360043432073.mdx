## Intro

Urban Airship helps engage mobile users and build high-value relationships. With the Urban Airship Connector, you'll get device and response data so you can be sure your mobile engagement strategy is on point. To learn more about the Urban Airship API, visit their page ([https://docs.urbanairship.com/api/ua/](https://docs.urbanairship.com/api/ua/ "https://docs.urbanairship.com/api/ua/") ).

You connect to your Urban Airship account in the Data Center. This topic discusses the fields and menus that are specific to the Urban Airship connector user interface. General information for adding DataSets, setting update schedules, and editing DataSet information is discussed in [Adding a DataSet Using a Data Connector](https://domo-support.domo.com/s/article/360042926274?language=en_US "Adding a DataSet Using a Data Connector").

## Prerequisites

To connect to your Urban Airship account and create a DataSet, you must have the following:

* An Urban Airship app key
* An Urban Airship app master secret
* The URL of your Urban Airship instance (e.g. https://go.urbanairship.com)

For information about finding your credentials, visit [https://support.urbanairship.com/hc/...or-App-Secret-](https://support.urbanairship.com/hc/en-us/articles/213491663-How-do-I-find-my-App-Key-or-App-Secret- "https://support.urbanairship.com/hc/en-us/articles/213491663-How-do-I-find-my-App-Key-or-App-Secret-").

## Connecting to Your Urban Airship Account

This section enumerates the options in the **Credentials** and **Details** panes on the Urban Airship Connector page. The components of the other panes on this page, **Scheduling**, and **Name & Describe Your DataSet**, are universal across most connector types and are discussed in greater length in [Adding a DataSet Using a Data Connector](https://domo-support.domo.com/s/article/360042926274?language=en_US "Adding a DataSet Using a Data Connector").
