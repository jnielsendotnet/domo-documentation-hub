### Intro

Welcome to Domo! Let's explore the key features and tools you'll use in your Domo instance. This guide will help you find and utilize the essential elements of Domo effectively.


---

### Dashboards

You can access your dashboards in the left navigation by selecting **Features** > **Dashboards**.

# Manage Dashboards

From the **Dashboards** side panel, select **Manage Dashboards** to create new dashboards or customize the order of existing dashboards. Remember, your arrangement doesn't affect other users.

<Frame><img alt="" src="/images/kb/ka0Vq000000GQhh-00N5w00000Ri7BU-0EMVq000008gNJl.jpg" style={{width: 246, height: 561}}/></Frame>
#### Create a Sub-Dashboard

Sub-dashboards add a layer of organization to the dashboard view. To create a sub-dashboard from the **Manage Dashboards** modal, select the arrow next to a top-level dashboard and select **Add a sub-Dashboard**.

<Frame><img alt="" src="/images/kb/ka0Vq000000GQhh-00N5w00000Ri7BU-0EMVq000008gO7l.jpg" style={{width: 500, height: 453}}/></Frame>

### Data

After you bring your data into Domo, you can access it in the DataSets feature. From the left navigation, select **Features** > **DataSets**.

<Frame><img alt="" src="/images/kb/ka0Vq000000GQhh-00N5w00000Ri7BU-0EMVq000008gKqY.jpg" style={{width: 900, height: 515}}/></Frame>
Going to DataFlows in the left navigation (**Features** > **DataFlows**) allows you to use ETL (Extract, Transform, and Load) processes, which are crucial for preparing your data.

<Frame><img alt="" src="/images/kb/ka0Vq000000GQhh-00N5w00000Ri7BU-0EMVq000008gJxj.jpg" style={{width: 900, height: 523}}/></Frame>
### Alerts

You can configure Alerts to notify you about significant changes, like a 20 percent drop in sales. They can be sent via email, text, or via the Domo app.\

Learn more about [Alerts](https://domo-support.domo.com/s/article/360043430373).

<Frame><img alt="" src="/images/kb/ka0Vq000000GQhh-00N5w00000Ri7BU-0EMVq000008gAEU.jpg" style={{width: 799, height: 450}}/></Frame>
