---
title: "CSV Upload Connector"
---

This connector is now DEPRECATED.

To upload CSV files from your local machine or network to Domo, use our [File Upload connector](/s/article/360043436573 "File Upload Connector").

To upload CSV files to Domo from an external SFTP server, use either our [CSV Advanced Connector](/s/article/360043436513 "CSV-SFTP Connector").

To upload CSV files to Domo using Domo's own SFTP server, use the [CSV SFTP Push Connector](/s/article/360042931914 "CSV SFTP Push Connector").

To upload CSV files to Domo from a website, use the [CSV Advanced Connector](/s/article/360043436513 "CSV Advanced Connector").

To upload CSV files to Domo from a third-party service, use the connector corresponding to that service, such as [Amazon S3](/s/article/360043436393 "Box Connector"), etc.
