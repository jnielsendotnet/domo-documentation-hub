## Intro

[Data.ai](http://Data.ai) is a business intelligence company and analyst firm that produces a range of business intelligence tools and market reports for the apps and digital goods industry. Use Domo's [Data.ai](http://Data.ai) connector to compile reports about accounts, products, advertising metrics, and the like. To learn more about the [Data.ai](http://Data.ai) API, visit their website ([https://support.data.ai.com/hc/en-u...-Analytics-API](https://support.appannie.com/hc/en-us/sections/204363108-App-Analytics-API "https://support.data.ai.com/hc/en-us/sections/204363108-App-Analytics-API") ).

You connect to your [Data.ai](http://Data.ai) account in the Data Center. This topic discusses the fields and menus that are specific to the [Data.ai](http://Data.ai) connector user interface. General information for adding DataSets, setting update schedules, and editing DataSet information is discussed in [Adding a DataSet Using a Data Connector](https://domo-support.domo.com/s/article/360042926274?language=en_US "Adding a DataSet Using a Data Connector").

## Prerequisites

To connect to your [Data.ai](http://Data.ai) account and create a DataSet, you must have the email address you use to log into [Data.ai](http://Data.ai), as well as your [Data.ai](http://Data.ai) password.

## Connecting to Your Data.ai Account

This section enumerates the options in the **Credentials** and **Details** panes in the [Data.ai](http://Data.ai) Connector page. The components of the other panes in this page, **Scheduling** and **Name & Describe Your DataSet**, are universal across most connector types and are discussed in greater length in [Adding a DataSet Using a Data Connector](https://domo-support.domo.com/s/article/360042926274?language=en_US "Adding a DataSet Using a Data Connector").

## Credentials Pane

The Domo [Data.ai](http://Data.ai) connector uses OAuth to connect, so there is no need to enter credentials within Domo. Click **Connect** (or select **Add Account** if you have existing [Data.ai](http://Data.ai) accounts in Domo) to open the [Data.ai](http://Data.ai) OAuth screen where you can enter your [Data.ai](http://Data.ai) credentials. Once you have entered valid credentials, you can use the same account any time you go to create a new [Data.ai](http://Data.ai) DataSet. You can manage connector accounts in the **Accounts** tab in the Data Center. For more information about this tab, see [Managing User Accounts for Connectors](https://domo-support.domo.com/s/article/360042926054?language=en_US "Managing User Accounts for Connectors").

<Note>
**Note:** If you are already logged into [Data.ai](http://Data.ai) when you connect in Domo, you are authenticated automatically when you click **Add account**. If you want to connect to an account that is different from the one you are logged into, you must first log out of Facebook or switch accounts (using the **Switch Accounts** option in the OAuth screen).
</Note>
