---
title: "Change the Owner of a DataSet"
---


### Intro

DataSets can be owned by one or more individuals or by a group. When you add a DataSet to Domo, you are the default owner of that DataSet. For most DataSet types, you can change the owner of a DataSet in its Details view.

Notes:

* If you don't own a DataSet, you must have an Admin system role or a custom role with the Manage DataSet grant enabled to change its owner. Learn more about
  [system roles](/s/article/360043438973).
* When someone is removed from Domo, their Domo data must be reassigned. See
  [Remove Users from Domo](/s/article/360042934334 "Removing Users from Domo") to learn more.
* Learn how to
  [add connector DataSets to Domo](/s/article/360042926274).

---

### Change DataSet Owner

DataSets can be owned by an individual or a group. Follow these steps to change the owner of a DataSet:

1. Locate the DataSet you want to reassign in the Data Center.
2. Select the DataSet to open its Details view.
3. Select
