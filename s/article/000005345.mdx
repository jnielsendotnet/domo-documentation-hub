## Intro

When your Google Campaign Manager data is integrated to Domo, the click-through rates and conversions improves significantly.

The **Google Campaign Manager Connector** eases your advertising campaign management, from media planning to reporting. The Google Campaign Manager Connector enables you to combine your Campaign Manager data with any other marketing data and get a holistic view of the effectiveness of your ad campaign. You can get a quick interactive view of your ad-serving options, click-through rates, conversions, and revenue, through a single real-time dashboard. With real-time data quickly available, you can better allocate your digital marketing budget to maximize your Return On Investment.

To learn more about the Google Campaign Manager API, visit their page ([Google Campaign Manager Connector](https://developers.google.com/doubleclick-advertisers/rest/v4 "Google Campaign Manager Connector")).

You can connect to your Google Campaign Manager account in the Data Center. This article explains the fields and menus specific to the Google Campaign Manager connector user interface. To add DataSets, set update schedules, and edit DataSet information, see [Adding a DataSet Using a Data Connector](https://domo-support.domo.com/s/article/360042926274 "Adding a DataSet Using a Data Connector").

## Prerequisites

To connect to the Google Campaign Manager account and create a DataSet, you must have the following:

* Google email address
* Password

## Connect to Your Google Campaign Manager Account

This section enumerates the options available in the **Credentials** and **Details** panes in the Google Campaign Manager Connector page. The components of the other panes in this page, **Scheduling** and **Name & Describe Your DataSet**, are universal across most connector types and are discussed in great detail in [Adding a DataSet Using a Data Connector](https://domo-support.domo.com/s/article/360042926274 "Adding a DataSet Using a Data Connector").

## Credentials Pane

Th Google Campaign Manager connector uses OAuth to connect, so there is no need to enter credentials within Domo. Select **Connect** (or select **Add Account** if you have existing Google accounts in Domo) to open the Google OAuth screen where you can enter your Google email address and password. Once you have entered the valid Google credentials, you can use the same account any time to create a new Google Campaign Manager DataSet. You can manage connector accounts in the **Accounts** tab in the Data Center. For more information about this tab, see [Managing User Accounts for Connectors](https://domo-support.domo.com/s/article/360042926054 "Managing User Accounts for Connectors").

**Note:** If you are already logged into Google when you connect to Domo, then your account will automatically get authenticated when you select **Add account**. If you want to connect to an account that is different from the one you are logged into, you must first log out of Google.
