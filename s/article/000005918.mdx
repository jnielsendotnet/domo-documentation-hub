### Intro

This article explains Domo AI Pro—the advanced tier of Domo AI, offering customizable, consumption-based AI tools. Discover the features included in Domo AI Pro and where to find more information about them.

<Note>
  **Note:** [AI Chat](https://domo-support.domo.com/s/article/000005539?language=en_US) will continue to operate at its current rate until it is integrated into AI Pro.

</Note>


---

### Required Grants

To use AI Pro features, ensure that your role includes the following grant:

* **Use AI Services** — Allows access to Domo AI and AI Pro features.

Learn more about [grants](https://domo-support.domo.com/s/article/360043438973?language=en_US).

### Security and Privacy

Domo AI Pro adheres to the same security and privacy standards as all Domo AI products. AI models run inside Domo's secure infrastructure, and data used in prompts or model interactions remains within your Domo instance. Learn more about [Domo AI's security and privacy standards](https://web-assets.domo.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2024/09/Domo.AI-and-DomoGPT-Security-and-Privacy-FAQ-Sept2024.pdf).

### Domo AI Pro

Domo AI Pro includes advanced features that you can scale and customize for your organization's specific needs. These AI tools utilize a transparent, consumption-based pricing model, which is clearly outlined in the [Rate Card tab](https://domo-support.domo.com/s/article/000005326?language=en_US#rate-card-tab) of your Credit Utilization page.

Frequently used Domo AI features—including [Beast Mode AI Assist](https://domo-support.domo.com/s/article/000005304?language=en_US) and [AI SQL Assistant](https://domo-support.domo.com/s/article/360044258533?language=en_US#ai_sql_assistant) —remain free as part of your Domo contract.

<Warning>
  **Important:** Domo AI Pro is subject to a consumption-based AI Operation charge plus token usage.

</Warning>

Domo AI Pro includes:

* **Magic ETL Text Generation tile** — Generate text outputs, summaries, or insights as part of your Magic ETL flow.
    
  Use AI Pro to [generate text in Magic ETL](https://domo-support.domo.com/s/article/000005741?language=en_US#text_generation_tile).
* **Magic ETL Vector Output tile** — Generate vector images and visual assets as part of your Magic ETL flow.
