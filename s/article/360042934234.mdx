title: "Admin Settings"
---

### Intro

The **Admin** tab of the **Features** menu allows you to access controls for many administrative functions in Domo, including managing users and groups, controlling user authentication, and managing settings for the AI Service Layer.

---

### Access the Admin Tab

Go to the **Admin** tab (wrench icon) in the left navigation. Your view of the tab depends on your level of Domo access.
