## Intro

Dynamics 365 Business Central provides a comprehensive view of your business, delivering real-time insights on every operational aspect. It streamlines processes across departments such as finance, sales, inventory, manufacturing, project management, and so on. This connector enables you to retrieve data from Dynamics 365 Business Central and load into Domo for analysis and visualization. The connector allows you to select any Business Central entity that has been exposed to web services to retrieve data. You can also schedule the data to be updated at regular intervals.

You can connect to your Dynamics 365 Business Central account in the Data Center. This topic discusses the fields and menus that are specific to the Dynamics 365 Business Central connector user interface. To add add DataSets, set update schedules, and edit DataSet information, see [Adding a DataSet Using a Data Connector](https://domo-support.domo.com/s/article/360042926274?language=en_US "Adding a DataSet Using a Data Connector").

## Prerequisites

To connect to your Dynamics 365 Business Central account and create a DataSet, you must have the following:

* Microsoft Entra ID
* Client ID
* Client Secret

To complete the Dynamics 365 Business Central registration process in the Azure portal, follow the below steps:

1. Log into the Azure portal (https://portal.azure.com/) using the account credentials.
2. Click the **App registrations** tile.
   
   <Frame><img src="/images/kb/ka0Vq00000010fV-00N5w00000Ri7BU-0EMVq000001Ht9B.jpg" style={{width: 725, height: 155}}/></Frame>

3. In the App registrations window, click
   **+ New registration** and enter the details.
   
   <Frame><img src="/images/kb/ka0Vq00000010fV-00N5w00000Ri7BU-0EMVq000001HtCP.jpg" style={{width: 730, height: 127}}/></Frame>

4. In the Register an application window, do the following:

   1. In the **Name** field enter, **Dynamics365BusinessCentral**.
   2. Under **Supported account types**, select the **Accounts in any organizational directory (Any Microsoft Entra ID tenant - Multitenant) and personal Microsoft accounts (e.g. Skype, Xbox)** radio button.
   3. From the **Redirect URI (optional)** drop-down, select **Web** and add enter the URL as [https://oauth.domo.com/api/data/v1/oauth/providers/dynamics-365-business-central/exchange.](https://oauth.domo.com/api/data/v1/oauth/providers/dynamics-365-business-central/exchange)
   4. Click the **Register** button.

   The registered app details appears along with the **Application (client) ID** and the **Directory (tenant) ID**, when you select the Overview section.

   <Frame><img src="/images/kb/ka0Vq00000010fV-00N5w00000Ri7BU-0EMVq000001HtM5.jpg" style={{width: 737, height: 459}}/></Frame>

5. On the left pane, click **Manage** > **Certificates & secrets** > **Client secrets** > **+ New client secret**.
   
   The **Add a client secret** window opens.
6. Enter the Description as
   **Domo Client Secret**, and from the drop-down list, select **Expires to 24 months**.
7. Click
   **Add**.
8. A new client secret gets created. Ensure to record the secret
   **Value**.
   
   <Frame><img src="/images/kb/ka0Vq00000010fV-00N5w00000Ri7BU-0EMVq000001HtPJ.jpg" style={{width: 741, height: 372}}/></Frame>

9. Make a note of the Application (client) ID, Directory (tenant) ID and the Client secrets Value.
10. On the left pane, click
    **Manage** > **API permissions** > **+ Add a permission**.
      
    <Frame><img src="/images/kb/ka0Vq00000010fV-00N5w00000Ri7BU-0EMVq000001HtSX.jpg" style={{width: 741, height: 397}}/></Frame>

11. Log in to the customer D365FO and navigate to,
    **System Administration** > **Setup** > **Microsoft Entra ID applications**.
12. Click **New**, and provide the ClientID from the Azure portal obtained from the Azure Active Directory applications form, and click **Save**.
      
    The Dynamics 365 Business Central registration process in the Azure Portal is successfully completed.
