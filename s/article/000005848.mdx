## New Features and Enhancements

### App Studio: New Table Element

Data tables in App Studio are now more intuitive, allowing you to explore and manipulate data like in a spreadsheet.

New functionalities include:

* Formatting
* Grouping
* Aggregating
* Sorting
* Resize columns
* Show/hide/reorder columns
* Add new calculation

Learn about [table elements](/s/article/000005681).

<Frame><img alt="" src="/images/kb/ka0Vq00000072GP-00N5w00000Ri7BU-0EMVq000003UCWf.jpg" style={{width: 895, height: 560}}/></Frame>

### Cloud Amplifier: Snowflake Configurable DataSet Level Location Information

The Snowflake configurable DataSet level location information improves governance by allowing you to move basic information between databases and schemas.

Functionalities include:

* Ability to change the database and/or schema of Domo-written DataSet.
* Ability to rename tables that were written by Domo to customer clouds.

Learn more about [Magic ETL on Snowflake](/s/article/000005455).

### Domo AI

#### AI Agents in Workflows

The AI Agent task in Workflows allows you to select and combine various AI tools to execute complex processes in a single step. Along with AI tools, your task can include tools like Code Engine functions and other workflows. The AI Agent appropriately identifies the correct tools for your request based on the data and details you provide.

<Frame><img alt="" src="/images/kb/ka0Vq000000CPgz-00N5w00000Ri7BU-0EMVq000005sfw9.jpg" style={{width: 900, height: 513}}/></Frame>

#### AI Content Builder Agent

The AI Content Builder Agent inspects your data for key insight categories and statistical insights and suggests cards to add to a dashboard or app, making it easy to gain more knowledge about your data.

#### AI Service for Deep Research

The AI Service for Deep Research uses AI to explore topics on your behalf and provides you with findings in a comprehensive, easy-to-read report, saving you time when completing complex research tasks.

#### AI Service Image-to-Text

The AI Service for Image-to-Text extracts text from an image, picture, or description, allowing you to quickly collect metadata from image files.

#### Embedded AI Chat

You can now enable AI Chat on privately embedded dashboards and apps. Subscribers have greater access to explore provided data.

<Frame><img alt="" src="/images/kb/ka0Vq000000CPgz-00N5w00000Ri7BU-0EMVq000005qZYH.jpg" style={{width: 895, height: 458}}/></Frame>
Learn more about [AI Chat](https://domo-support.domo.com/s/article/000005539?language=en_US).

### In Product Education Materials

Educational headers are now available across many features throughout the product, including Domo AI, DataFlows, Workflows, and App Studio, to improve the quality and accessibility of our training content. Most education content can be accessed directly from the feature and viewed within the product without opening a new tab or browser. Content includes specific examples, use cases, and quick links to more training content.

### Magic ETL

#### Coarser Grid

The new Coarser Grid Canvas setting allows you to add and enable various grid lines to your Magic ETL canvas, enabling you to organize your ETL more easily.

Learn how to [turn on the coarser grid](https://domo-support.domo.com/s/article/360055259234?language=en_US#coarser_grid) while configuring your Magic ETL DataFlow.

<Frame><img alt="" src="/images/kb/ka0Vq000000CPgz-00N5w00000Ri7BU-0EMVq000005Rlcg.jpg" style={{width: 619, height: 428}}/></Frame>
#### SQL Action

The Magic ETL SQL tile allows you to write your own freeform SQL in the Magic canvas, giving you the ultimate flexibility to transform your data however you choose. You can use SQL or a combination of SQL and drag-and-drop tiles to complete your transformation. You can also open an existing SQL DataFlow as a Magic ETL DataFlow with pre-written SQL tiles for each SQL transform in the DataFlow.

Learn how to [configure and use the SQL tile](/s/article/360044876874#sql_tile) in Magic ETL.

<Frame><img alt="" src="/images/kb/ka0Vq000000CPgz-00N5w00000Ri7BU-0EMVq000005sc8h.jpg" style={{width: 699, height: 417}}/></Frame>
#### Tile Sections and Sticky Notes

Tile Sections allow you to create sections of logically and visually grouped tiles in your Magic ETL DataFlow. Sticky Notes help you organize your DataFlow, including context for viewers about what is happening in the DataFlow or instructions for work that may still need to be done. You can add Sticky Notes to Tile Sections or directly to the Magic ETL canvas.

You can customize Tile Sections and Sticky Notes with rich text, including color, font size, and more.

Learn how to add [sections](https://domo-support.domo.com/s/article/360055259234?language=en_US#use_sections) and [sticky notes](https://domo-support.domo.com/s/article/360055259234?language=en_US#use_sticky_notes) to your Magic ETL DataFlows.

<Frame><img alt="" src="/images/kb/ka0Vq000000CPgz-00N5w00000Ri7BU-0EMVq000005T0g6.jpg" style={{width: 826, height: 509}}/></Frame>
#### Undo/Redo Capabilities

The new Undo/Redo capabilities in Magic ETL allow you to undo changes you've made to the canvas and redo changes you've undone.

This update includes a history list where you can jump to different actions and view the DataFlow in that state.

<Frame><img alt="" src="/images/kb/ka0Vq000000CPgz-00N5w00000Ri7BU-0EMVq000005THsD.jpg" style={{width: 487, height: 406}}/></Frame>
Learn how to [create a Magic ETL DataFlow](/s/article/360055259234).

### Power Up Multiple Connector DataSets

When adding a DataSet to Domo via a connector, the new ability to power up multiple connector DataSets allows you to save a new DataSet and then return to the power-up screen for the same connector. This reduces the amount of time spent adding multiple DataSets with the same connector.

<Frame><img alt="" src="/images/kb/ka0Vq000000CPgz-00N5w00000Ri7BU-0EMVq000005TcDV.jpg" style={{width: 658, height: 252}}/></Frame>
### Workflows

#### DomoStats for Workflows

<Frame><img alt="" src="/images/kb/ka0Vq000000CPgz-00N5w00000Ri7BU-0EMVq000005Rf5p.jpg" style={{width: 93, height: 94}}/></Frame>
The Workflows DomoStats report captures details for each executed workflow in an instance.

The following information is available in the Workflows DomoStat report:

* Workflow name
* Workflow version
* Execution ID
* Model ID
* Trigger type
* Trigger ID
* Created by
* Started by
* Deployed by
* Workflow status
* Start time
* End time
* Cycle time
* Qualifying tasks

Learn more about [DomoStats](/s/article/360043433813) and the [Workflows](https://domo-support.domo.com/s/article/360043433813?language=en_US#workflows_dataset) report.

#### Jupyter Notebook Integration for Workflows

You can now call Jupyter Notebook functions as tasks in Workflows. This integration allows you to find the package in the Workflows action menu and select the corresponding function to call any of the scripts you have completed in Jupyter Notebooks.

<Frame><img alt="" src="/images/kb/ka0Vq000000CPgz-00N5w00000Ri7BU-0EMVq000005qfDx.jpg" style={{width: 456, height: 540}}/></Frame>
Learn more about executing [Jupyter Notebooks in Workflows](https://domo-support.domo.com/s/article/36004740075?language=en_US#executions_via_workflows).

#### Workflow Triggers

Workflow Triggers allow you to trigger a workflow from other Domo products, including an App Studio or Pro-code app, another workflow, or an API. Triggers can only be configured after a workflow has been deployed, except for manual triggers.

Trigger integrations include:

* Alerts
* App Studio
* Pro-code apps
* Manual
* Workflows
* Timers
* APIs

Learn how to [configure and manage triggers](https://domo-support.domo.com/s/article/000005179?language=en_US#manage_triggers).

#### Report Builder for Scheduled Reports

The Report Builder in App Studio allows you to build customizable email reports using your apps.

Report Builder allows you to do the following:

* Include multiple app pages
* Drag, drop, swap, resize, and remove your content within the Report Builder
* Add additional content to enhance reports, including headers, borders, cards, and text
* Configuration options for customizing email headers and footers with logos and content
* Add a button that links to Domo content, including the app itself or a card/dashboard
* Add theme components, including card styles, background colors, and density, and import themes from any app to sync color palettes
* Add filters and schedule the report for users and groups

### Domo AI

#### AI Automated Insights & Metrics

AI Automated Insights monitor metrics you have configured and defined context for within Domo. Create a semantic model that describes relationships and builds context for your data to help the Automated Insights track and flag your DataSet metrics to create summary insights. Automated Insights also follow set user roles, priorities, and data access in your instance to ensure everyone receives the correct level of insight.

#### Domo AI FileSets

The Domo AI FileSets tool extracts and gathers structured information from images, documents, transcriptions, customer reviews, and other unstructured data to power visualizations and data products in Domo. FileSets also helps you automate document-driven business processes to streamline your business.

# Magic ETL

#### AI Text Generation Magic ETL Tile

The AI Text Generation Magic ETL tile leverages the power of Domo AI to process text fields in your data. The tile allows you to analyze and gain further value from previously inaccessible information in your DataSets.

### Schema and Meta Select Tiles

The Schema and Meta Select tiles allow you to dynamically select data as part of your DataFlows and act on schema information inside a DataSet from Magic ETL. Tile functionalities include the ability to rename columns dynamically, filter out columns based on a set column name or type, and modify data using expressions that apply to the whole column dynamically.
