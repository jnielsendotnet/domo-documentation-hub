## Intro

Use the DV 360 Connector to fetch digital advertising campaign information. To learn more about the DV 360 API, visit their page ([https://developers.google.com/display-video/api/guides/downloading-sdfs/create](https://developers.google.com/display-video/api/guides/downloading-sdfs/create "https://developers.google.com/display-video/api/guides/downloading-sdfs/create") ).

You connect to your DV 360 account in the Data Center. This topic discusses the fields and menus that are specific to the DV 360 connector user interface. To add DataSets, set update schedules, and edit DataSet information, see [Adding a DataSet Using a Connector](https://domo-support.domo.com/s/article/360042926274?language=en_US "Adding a DataSet Using a Connector").

## Prerequisites

To connect to your DV 360 account and create a DataSet, you must have the email address or phone number and the password associated with your DV 360 account.

## Connecting to Your DV 360 Account

This section enumerates the options in the **Credentials** and **Details** panes on the DV 360 Connector page. The components of the other panes on this page, **Scheduling**, and **Name & Describe Your DataSet**, are universal across most connector types and are discussed in great detail in [Adding a DataSet Using a Connector](https://domo-support.domo.com/s/article/360042926274?language=en_US "Adding a DataSet Using a Connector").

## Credentials Pane

The DV 360 connector uses OAuth 2.0 for authentication. Once you have entered valid DV 360 credentials, you can use the same account at any time to create a new DV 360 DataSet. You can manage connector accounts in the **Accounts** tab in the Data Center. For more information about this tab, see [Manage Connector Accounts](https://domo-support.domo.com/s/article/360042926054?language=en_US "Manage Connector Accounts").
