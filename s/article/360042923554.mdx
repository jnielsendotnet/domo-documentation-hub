---
title: "Domo Mobile Best Practices"
---

### Intro

The following are recommended best practices for using the Domo Mobile app.

---

### Home Screen

Add your most important dashboards to your Home screen by long pressing on any tile.

### Collections

Organize your collections and cards from most to least importance.

### Summary Numbers

Make sure Summary Numbers are short and easy to read.

<Warning>
  **Important:** HTML is NOT supported in Summary Numbers for mobile.
</Warning>

### Cards

Avoid cards that are too granular or busy.

### Chart Types

Tap into interactive mode and rotate your device to view different chart types.

### Leverage Drill Downs

Double-tap on a chart to "drill down" to see filtered views.

### Alerts

Set up Domo Alerts on your mobile device by following these steps:

1. Go to **Settings**.
2. Select **Notifications**.
3. Select **Domo App**.
4. Choose your preferred alert settings.

### Notes

* Long press on your menu button to move it anywhere on the screen.
* Cards created in Analyzer will be responsive on mobile devices.
* Custom apps must be designed for specific screen sizes.

<Note>
  **Chart Properties:** Occasionally, chart properties are added to the Web client to enhance the user experience when building cards. These features are not always supported in the mobile app. For example, in the web version, you can select where you want the "Total" row to reside by configuring the "Total Row Position" property. On mobile, the Total row is currently only available at the bottom. As another example, you can choose “Show Group Name Once” on the Web, but in mobile, this feature is not recognized. We are reviewing these and other features currently available in the web version for future enhancement to the mobile experience.
</Note>
