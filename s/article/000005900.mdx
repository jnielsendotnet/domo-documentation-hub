### Intro

 This article explains how to find and start creating content through Domo's navigation bar and main menu and how to personalize your navigation experience.


---

### Access Domo's Navigation

 You can find Domo Navigation on the left side of your browser window from every screen in Domo—except full-screen editors like those in Workflows or App Studio.
 
<Frame><img src="/images/kb/ka0Vq000000IS4H-00N5w00000Ri7BU-0EMVq000009aHBd.jpg" /></Frame>
 By default, the navigation displays only the icon for each item.
 
 To expand the navigation bar and show all icons with their titles, select the Domo icon at the bottom of the navigation or any empty space within the navigation bar.
 
<Frame><img src="/images/kb/ka0Vq000000IS4H-00N5w00000Ri7BU-0EMVq000009aHEr.jpg" style={{width: 181, height: 497}}/></Frame>
### Enable Navigation in Your Instance

 If you are a **Domo Administrator**  , you can enable or disable the navigation for all users or a specific group of users. Learn more about the [Domo administrator role](https://domo-support.domo.com/s/article/360043438953?language=en_US#admin)  .
 
 To enable Domo Navigation in your instance, follow these steps:
 
1. Navigate to the
 **Admin**  menu.
2. Under
 **Features**  , select **Feature management**  .
   
<Frame><img src="/images/kb/ka0Vq000000IS4H-00N5w00000Ri7BU-0EMVq000009aHYD.jpg" style={{width: 285, height: 283}}/></Frame>
3. Choose one of the following settings:
	1. **Not enabled in your instance**
		— Disables the navigation for all users.
	2. **Enabled for everyone** 
		— Enables the navigation for everyone in your instance.
	3. **Enabled for admins** 
		— Enables the new navigation for administrators only.
	4. **Enabled for a specific group** 
		— Enables the navigation for a specific Domo group.
			
		You can create a group with a specific subset of users to enable navigation, or you can remove them from a group to exclude them.
			
	<Frame><img alt="A screenshot of a computerAI-generated content may be incorrect." src="/images/kb/ka0Vq000000IS4H-00N5w00000Ri7BU-0EMVq000009aHzd.jpg" style={{width: 352, height: 281}}/></Frame>
4. Select
 **Save**  to apply your enablement settings.

### Lock the Navigation Bar

 When the Domo Navigation is unpinned, it hovers over the rest of the screen.
 
 When you select the pin icon in the left corner of the expanded navigation, it stays in the fixed location and does not cover any part of the screen.
 
 To lock and unlock the expanded state of the navigation, select the pin icon <InlineImage src="/images/kb/ka0Vq000000IS4H-00N5w00000Ri7BU-0EMVq000009ZzMo.jpg" /> in the top right corner of the navigation bar.

### Access and Create Content

 In the Domo navigation bar, you can view your instance's logo, name, and URL, access and create content through the main menu, find your pinned items, and view your user profile.
 
#### Find Your Instance's Logo, Name, and URL

 Your instance's logo, name, and URL display at the top of the navigation.
 
<Frame><img src="/images/kb/ka0Vq000000IS4H-00N5w00000Ri7BU-0EMVq000009aIxJ.jpg" style={{width: 276, height: 147}}/></Frame>
#### Navigate the Main Menu

 You can navigate Domo's features, create new content, view your most recent content, and access administrative tools in the main menu.
 
<Frame><img alt="A screenshot of a phoneAI-generated content may be incorrect." src="/images/kb/ka0Vq000000IS4H-00N5w00000Ri7BU-0EMVq000009aK1R.jpg" style={{width: 252, height: 246}}/></Frame>
##### Find Features

 Select **Features**  to find all of Domo's functionality by category.
 
<Frame><img alt="A screenshot of a computerAI-generated content may be incorrect." src="/images/kb/ka0Vq000000IS4H-00N5w00000Ri7BU-0EMVq000009aMrF.jpg" style={{width: 437, height: 451}}/></Frame>
##### Search for Content

 Locate any content in your Domo instance by typing a keyword into the **Search**  bar. You also have the option to browse for content by specific feature.
 
 <Note>
   **Note:** 
   The navigation's search function prioritizes content you have previously visited.

 </Note>

<Frame><img alt="A screenshot of a computerAI-generated content may be incorrect." src="/images/kb/ka0Vq000000IS4H-00N5w00000Ri7BU-0EMVq000009aMUg.jpg" style={{width: 624, height: 642}}/></Frame>
##### Create New Content

 Select **Add**  to access the **Create**  menu and build new assets such as App Studio apps, cards, datasets, workflows, and tasks. You can also connect and transform data, manage users and groups, and start conversations from this menu.
 
<Frame><img alt="A screenshot of a computerAI-generated content may be incorrect." src="/images/kb/ka0Vq000000IS4H-00N5w00000Ri7BU-0EMVq000009aNKH.jpg" style={{width: 624, height: 278}}/></Frame>
##### View Recent Content

 Select **Recent**  to access content and features you've worked on lately, organized by timeframe.
 
<Frame><img alt="A screenshot of a computerAI-generated content may be incorrect." src="/images/kb/ka0Vq000000IS4H-00N5w00000Ri7BU-0EMVq000009aNXB.jpg" style={{width: 624, height: 305}}/></Frame>
##### Access Administrative Settings

 Select **Admin**  to navigate all administrative tools, including governance, security, and feature management.
 
<Frame><img alt="A screenshot of a computerAI-generated content may be incorrect." src="/images/kb/ka0Vq000000IS4H-00N5w00000Ri7BU-0EMVq000009aNfF.jpg" style={{width: 472, height: 484}}/></Frame>
#### Find Your Pinned Items

 When you pin an item, such as a Domo feature, App Studio app, card, or link, it shows in the pinned items section of the navigation. Learn how to [pin items to your Domo navigation](#pin-items)  .
 
 <Note>
   **Note:** 
   When you pin multiple items, the navigation bar automatically adjusts. If the space is filled, you can access all your pinned items from a sub-menu, indicated by the
   **All navigation items** <InlineImage src="/images/kb/ka0Vq000000IS4H-00N5w00000Ri7BU-0EMVq000009aLLh.jpg" />
   icon. Select this icon to view the complete list and navigate to any of your pinned assets.

 </Note>

#### Open Your Profile

 You can find personal settings and activity, including **Account Details**  , **Notifications**  , and **Sign Out,**  by selecting the **User Profile**  icon at the bottom of the navigation.
 
<Frame><img src="/images/kb/ka0Vq000000IS4H-00N5w00000Ri7BU-0EMVq000009aLbp.jpg" style={{width: 202, height: 525}}/></Frame>
### Personalize Your Navigation Experience

 Pin your most-used features, like App Studio or DataFlows, or actions, like uploading a file or opening a card, to your Pinned Items navigation below the main menu.

#### Pin Items

 You can add any feature in the main menu to your pinned items by hovering over then selecting the **Add to navigation**  icon <InlineImage src="/images/kb/ka0Vq000000IS4H-00N5w00000Ri7BU-0EMVq000009aNiT.jpg" />.
 
 You can pin any number of items to your pinned items section. The new navigation scrolls as you add more items.

#### Edit/Unpin Items

 You can edit the details for any item in your pinned items by accessing that item's pin settings.
 
1. Hover over the item and right-click it to open its pin settings.
   
<Frame><img alt="A screenshot of a computerAI-generated content may be incorrect." src="/images/kb/ka0Vq000000IS4H-00N5w00000Ri7BU-0EMVq000009aOD7.jpg" style={{width: 218, height: 137}}/></Frame>
2. Edit, remove, open, or reorder the item.
3. Select **Done** to save your changes.
