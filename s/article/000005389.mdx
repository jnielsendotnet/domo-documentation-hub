## About

Infusionsoft by Keap offers an e-mail marketing and sales platform for small businesses, including products, to streamline the customer lifecycle, customer relationship management, marketing automation, lead capture, and e-commerce. Use this connector to pull the details for product, orders and various e-commerce related properties.

You can connect to your Infusionsoft by Keap Rest account in the Data Center. This article explains the fields and menus that are specific to the Infusionsoft by Keap Rest Connector user interface. To add DataSets, set update schedules, and edit DataSet information, see [Adding a DataSet Using a Data Connector](https://domo-support.domo.com/s/article/360042926274?language=en_US "Adding a DataSet Using a Data Connector").

## Prerequisites

To connect to your Infusionsoft by Keap Rest account and create a DataSet, you must have the Email and Password associated with your Infusionsoft by Keap Rest account.

## Connecting to Your Infusionsoft by Keap Rest Account

This section lists the options in the **Credentials** and **Details** panes in the Infusionsoft by Keap Rest Connector page. The components of the other panes in this page, **Scheduling** and **Name & Describe Your DataSet**, are universal across most connector types and are discussed in great detail in [Adding a DataSet Using a Data Connector](https://domo-support.domo.com/s/article/360042926274?language=en_US "Adding a DataSet Using a Data Connector").

## Credentials Pane

You need the Email and Password associated with your Infusionsoft by Keap Rest account to connect to your Infusionsoft by Keap Rest connector. Once you have entered the valid Infusionsoft by Keap Rest credentials, you can use the same account to create a new Infusionsoft by Keap Rest DataSet. You can manage connector accounts in the **Accounts** tab in the Data Center. For more information about this tab, see [Managing User Accounts for Connectors](https://domo-support.domo.com/s/article/360042926054?language=en_US "Managing User Accounts for Connectors").
