## Intro

Domo’s improved accessibility functionality allows customers to make their cards screen reader-friendly, as well as find dashboards, traverse layouts, and read card details. Expanded access for screen readers and keyboards better enables customers with a diverse base of users to expose Domo content to users with accessibility requirements.
