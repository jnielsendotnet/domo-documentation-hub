## About

Use the **inContact CxOne OAuth Connector** to monitor your teams and agents performance and get the most out of your inContact data.

The inContact helps organizations to create the best customer experience when you meet key business metrics. Use Domo's inContact CxOne OAuth Connector to fetch the contacts, agents, skill summaries, campaigns, teams, and agents performance data while tracking agent and contact center performance.

By combining your inContact data with Domo's leading business intelligent platform, you will develop comprehensive strategies to completely optimize your business. Within minutes you will be able to create customized reports for the most important KPIs, which will help you get a holistic view of your inContact data. Creates powerful and engaging data visualizations so that you can quickly track, how your campaigns, teams, and agents are performing. To learn more about the inContact API, see [inContact CxOne OAuth API](https://developer.niceincontact.com/API "inContact CxOne OAuth API").

You can connect to your inContact account in the Data Center. This article explains the fields and menus that are specific to the inContact CxOne OAuth Connector user interface. To add DataSets, set update schedules, and edit DataSet information, see [Adding a DataSet Using a Connector](https://domo-support.domo.com/s/article/360042926274?language=en_US "Adding a DataSet Using a Connector").

## Prerequisites

To connect to your inContact CxOne OAuth account and create a DataSet, you must have the following:

* Client ID
* Client Secret
* Access Key
* Access Secret
* Tenant ID (optional)

## Connecting to Your inContact Account

This section lists the options in the **Credentials** and **Details** panes on the inContact CxOne OAuth Connector page. The components of the other panes on this page, **Scheduling**, and **Name & Describe Your DataSet**, are universal across most connector types and are discussed in great detail in [Adding a DataSet Using a Connector](https://domo-support.domo.com/s/article/360042926274?language=en_US "Adding a DataSet Using a Connector").

The inContact CxOne OAuth Connector uses OAuth 2 for authentication.
