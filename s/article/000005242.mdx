## Intro

Reddit is an entertainment, social networking service and news website where registered community members can submit content, such as text posts or direct links. Use Domo's Reddit Ads connector to get data your ads performance.

This topic discusses the fields and menus that are specific to the Reddit Ads Connector user interface. General information for adding DataSets, setting update schedules, and editing DataSet information is discussed in [Adding a DataSet Using a Connector](/s/article/360042926274 "Adding a DataSet Using a Connector").

## Prerequisites

To connect to your Reddit Ads account and create a DataSet, you must have the following:

* Your Reddit Ads account name and credentials.

## Connecting to Your Reddit Ads Account

This section enumerates the options in the Credentials and Details panes in the Reddit Ads Connector page. The components of the other panes in this page, **Scheduling** and **Name & Describe Your DataSet**, are universal across most connector types and are discussed in greater length in [Adding a DataSet Using a Connector](/s/article/360042926274 "Adding a DataSet Using a Connector").

### Credentials Pane

To access this connector, you need to login using your Reddit Ads username and password.

Once you have entered valid Reddit Ads credentials, you can use the same account any time you go to create a new Reddit Ads DataSet. You can manage Connector accounts in the **Accounts** tab in the Data Center. For more information about this tab, see [Manage Connector/Adapter Accounts](/s/article/360042926054 "Manage Connector/Adapter Accounts").

<Accordion title="What can I do with this connector?">

You can use this connector to get the data your ads performance.

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What is the request rate limit for this connector?">

Advertisers may perform 1 request per second.

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What is the limitation associated with this connector?">

All campaign management data is consistent. All reporting data is accurate up to three hours ago. That means fetching all impressions for today at 6 PM will pull all data up to 3 PM.

</Accordion>
