### Intro

Magic ETL is now more performant, consistent, and feature-rich. Magic ETL v2 is broadly compatible with Magic ETL v1; most existing transforms can be upgraded frictionlessly. You must upgrade Magic ETL for each DataFlow. Follow the instructions to upgrade below.

<Warning>
  **Important:**
  There are significant behavioral changes in Magic ETL which may affect your DataFlows. Learn about them in
  [Behavior Changes and Feature Updates in Magic ETL](/s/article/360047787514)
  before upgrading.

</Warning>

### Upgrade Magic ETL

Follow these steps for each DataFlow you want to upgrade:

1. In the Data Center, go to the DataFlow you want to upgrade.
2. Select
   **Edit** to open the DataFlow in Magic ETL.
     
   <Frame><img alt="edit dataflow.jpg" src="/images/kb/ka0Vq0000000l3l-00N5w00000Ri7BU-0EMVq000000nTSH.jpg" style={{width: 899, height: 240}}/></Frame>
3. At the top right corner of the canvas, toggle the switch to
   **Try the new Magic ETL**.
     
   <Frame><img alt="Try_the_new_Magic_ETL.png" src="/images/kb/ka0Vq0000000l3l-00N5w00000Ri7BU-0EM5w000005vOtw.png"/></Frame>
   <Warning>
     **WARNING:**
     After you upgrade a DataFlow, the toggle is no longer available and you cannot revert to Magic ETL v1.

   </Warning>
