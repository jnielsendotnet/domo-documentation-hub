---
title: "Best Practices for Creating Visualization Cards"
---

When building KPI cards in Domo, always adhere to the following practices:

* Be sure to include the following information in the
  [description](/s/article/360042924174 "Editing the Name or Description of a KPI Card"):

  + The business initiative
  + The card's strategic importance
  + Actions the user can take based on this data
  + Helpful
    [alerts](/s/article/360043430533 "Notifications and Alerts") the user can set
  + The date range
  + [Drilldown options](/s/article/360042924094 "Adding Drill Path to Your Chart") for the card
  + The refresh rate
  + Who the card is audited by
      
    <Frame><img alt="Card_Description.jpg" src="/images/kb/ka05w00000123fR-00N5w00000Ri7BU-0EM5w000005vOqB.jpg"/></Frame>
* Use an appropriate chart type with...

  + [A descriptive title](/s/article/360042924174 "Editing the Name or Description of a KPI Card")
  + [Named axes](/s/article/360042925174 "Adding Scale Titles to Your Chart")
  + Consistent
    [formatting](/s/article/360043439993 "Chart Properties") with other cards in the collection (for example, blue line = forecast, green bar = current, and so on)
  + A
    [goal line](/s/article/360043428773 "Adding a Goal Line to Your Chart") if there is a goal associated with the data
* Use an appropriate
  [summary number](/s/article/360043428753 "Configuring Your Chart Summary Number") for the data represented in the card.
* Ensure the card is
  [linked to other appropriate content](/s/article/360042923774 "Linking Related Cards") that applies to the same business question.
* Ensure the card is assigned to the appropriate
  [owner](/s/article/360042923794 "Changing a Card Owner") (the person responsible for answering questions about the card).
