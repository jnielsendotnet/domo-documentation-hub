## Intro

This connector helps to fetch the reach and engagement data (number of shares, likes, the duration of the video, the number of people who watched the complete video, and so on) for all the public videos posted from a TikTok account. To learn more, refer to the API documentation [https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1762228421622786](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1762228421622786).

You can connect to your TikTok Business account in the Data Center. This topic discusses the fields and menus specific to the TikTok Business connector user interface. To add DataSets, set update schedules, and edit DataSet information, see [Adding a DataSet Using a Connector](/s/article/360042926274).

## Prerequisites

You need your TikTok account details to connect to your TikTok Business account and create a DataSet.

## Connecting to Your TikTok Business Account

This section enumerates the options in the **Credentials** and **Details** panes on the TikTok Business Connector page. The components of the other panes on this page, **Scheduling**, and **Name & Describe Your DataSet**, are universal across most connector types and are discussed in great detail in [Adding a DataSet Using a Connector](/s/article/360042926274).

## Credentials Pane

Once you have entered valid TikTok Business credentials, you can use the same account at any time to create a new TikTok Business DataSet. You can manage connector accounts in the **Accounts** tab in the Data Center. For more information about this tab, see [Manage Connector Accounts](/s/article/360042926054).

## Details Pane

| Menu | Description |
