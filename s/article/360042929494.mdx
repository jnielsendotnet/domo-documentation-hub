## Intro

AppNexus Yieldex is a provider of digital ad forecasting, analytic pricing and marketplace tools for publishers and advertisers. To learn more about the AppNexus API, visit their page ([https://wiki.appnexus.com/display/api/Home](https://wiki.appnexus.com/display/api/Home "https://wiki.appnexus.com/display/api/Home") ).

You connect to your Yieldex account in the Data Center. This topic discusses the fields and menus that are specific to the AppNexus connector user interface. General information for adding DataSets, setting update schedules, and editing DataSet information is discussed in [Adding a DataSet Using a Data Connector](https://domo-support.domo.com/s/article/360042926274?language=en_US "Adding a DataSet Using a Data Connector").

## Prerequisites

To connect to your Yieldex account and create a DataSet, you must have the following:

* Yieldex Client URL
* Username
* Password
* Source

## Connecting to Your Yieldex Account

This section enumerates the options in the **Credentials** and **Details** panes in the Yieldex Connector page. The components of the other panes in this page, **Scheduling** and **Name & Describe Your DataSet**, are universal across most connector types and are discussed in greater length in [Adding a DataSet Using a Data Connector](https://domo-support.domo.com/s/article/360042926274?language=en_US "Adding a DataSet Using a Data Connector").
