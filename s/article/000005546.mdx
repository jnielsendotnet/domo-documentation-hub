## Intro

AD EBiS is one of the largest advertising analytics platforms in Japan. Use Domo's AD EBiS REST connector to gather data about impressions, clicks, and conversions.

You can connect to your AD EBiS REST account in the Data Center. This topic discusses the fields and menus that are specific to the AD EBiS REST connector user interface. To add DataSets, set update schedules, and edit DataSet information, see [Adding a DataSet Using a Connector](/s/article/360042926274 "Adding a DataSet Using a Connector").

## Prerequisites

To connect to your AD EBiS REST account and create a DataSet, you must have the following:

* Refresh Token

**Note:** You can contact the AD EBiS support desk to obtain your refresh token.

## Connecting to Your AD EBiS REST Account

This section enumerates the options in the **Credentials** and **Details** panes on the AD EBiS REST Connector page. The components of the other panes on this page, **Scheduling**, and **Name & Describe Your DataSet**, are universal across most connector types and are discussed in great detail in [Adding a DataSet Using a Connector](/s/article/360042926274 "Adding a DataSet Using a Connector").

## Credentials Pane

This pane contains fields for entering credentials to connect to your AD EBiS REST account. For information about obtaining these credentials, see the Prerequisites section. The following table describes what is needed for each field:
