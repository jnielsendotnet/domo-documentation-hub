<table border="1" cellpadding="1" cellspacing="1" data-aura-rendered-by="33:207;a"><thead><tr><th colspan="1" rowspan="1"><p> Term </p></th><th colspan="1" rowspan="1"><p> Definition </p></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td colspan="1" rowspan="1"><p> Gauge </p></td><td colspan="1" rowspan="1"><p> A chart type in which data values are classified as belonging to a predetermined range.
