### Access Company Settings

 Most of these tasks can be completed in the company settings. Follow these steps to access them:
 
1. Open the **Features**  menu in the left navigation and go to the **Admin**  tab.
<Frame><img alt="" src="/images/kb/ka0Vq000000FyQT-00N5w00000Ri7BU-0EMVq000008fZ5H.jpg" style={{width: 601, height: 116}}/></Frame>
2. Under
 **Company settings**  , select **Company** 
**overview**.



### Upload Your Company Logo

 Your company logo is a critical part of your Domo instance. It not only personalizes your experience but also assures users they are in the right place.
 
 Follow these steps:
   
1. [Access](#access_company_settings) 
 the company settings, described above.
2. Select
 **Manage Logo**  to choose a company logo.
   
<Frame><img alt="" src="/images/kb/ka0Vq000000FyQT-00N5w00000Ri7BU-0EMVq000008fhAs.jpg" style={{width: 750, height: 532}}/></Frame>
3. Save your changes using the
 **Save**  option on the **Company overview**  page.
   
<Frame><img alt="" src="/images/kb/ka0Vq000000FyQT-00N5w00000Ri7BU-0EMVq000008fjCf.jpg" style={{width: 897, height: 268}}/></Frame>
 You can also enter your company's name and choose a default language on this screen.


 
### Set Your Time Zone

 Correctly setting your time zone is crucial for scheduling and data triggers.
 
 <Note>
   **Note:** 
   Setting your timezone only changes items in the Domo platform. It does not change any raw data coming into Domo.

 </Note>

 Follow these steps:
   
1. [Access](#access_company_settings) 
 the company settings, described above.
2. Under
 **Formatting**  , choose your time zone.
   
<Frame><img alt="" src="/images/kb/ka0Vq000000FyQT-00N5w00000Ri7BU-0EMVq000008fguk.jpg" style={{width: 496, height: 113}}/></Frame>
### Configure Week Dates

 Week dates influence how your charts and analyses display data by week.
 
 Follow these steps:
   
1. [Access](#access_company_settings) 
 the company settings, described above.
2. Scroll to the bottom of the page and use the
 **Week display**  dropdown to choose your preference.
	1. **Week dates —** 
	 1/15/2001
	2. **Week numbers —** 
	 (Week 1, Week 2, and so on)

### Add Security with Domain Restrictions

<Note>
  **Optional Domain Security:** 

  For added security, you can restrict user access to your Domo instance based on email domains. That way, only users who have a verified email address from a specific domain can be added to your Domo instance.

</Note>

* Follow these steps to configure domain restrictions:
   
1. Open the
 **Features**  menu in the left navigation and go to the **Admin**  tab.
   
<Frame><img alt="" src="/images/kb/ka0Vq000000FyQT-00N5w00000Ri7BU-0EMVq000008fZ5H-1.jpg" style={{width: 601, height: 116}}/></Frame>
2. Under
 **Network security**  , select **Invite authorized domains**  .
3. Enter the approved domains. Only email addresses from these domains can join your Domo instance.
   
<Frame><img alt="" src="/images/kb/ka0Vq000000FyQT-00N5w00000Ri7BU-0EMVq000008ftgf.jpg" style={{width: 599, height: 271}}/></Frame>
### Invite Users and Assign Roles

 Inviting colleagues to your Domo instance and assigning appropriate roles is a fundamental step for success.
 
 Follow these steps:
   
1. Open the
 **Features**  menu in the left navigation and go to the **Admin**  tab.
   
<Frame><img alt="" src="/images/kb/ka0Vq000000FyQT-00N5w00000Ri7BU-0EMVq000008fZ5H-2.jpg" style={{width: 601, height: 116}}/></Frame>
2. Under
 **Governance**, select **People**.
3. Select
 **Add New Person**  and add their name and email address.
4. Choose an appropriate role for them. Editor is generally a good starting point for new users. To learn more about the available roles, see the
 [System Roles | Reference](https://domo-support.domo.com/s/article/360043438953).


<Frame><img alt="" src="/images/kb/ka0Vq000000FyQT-00N5w00000Ri7BU-0EMVq000008fukn.jpg" style={{width: 499, height: 512}}/></Frame>
 The person you added now displays in the list.
