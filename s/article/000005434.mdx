## Intro

SAP is a market-leading cloud business software company that provides platforms for ERP, CRM, analytics, and more. The Domo SAP connector makes it easy to bring all your data from SAP into Domo.This connector helps you use Domo in conjunction with your SAP data to get a centralized view of your most mission-critical data.
  
Get faster, more relevant insights to help drive your business forward, with real-time data that is available to any team member on any device. You can also set up custom alerts to be notified instantly when your key metrics deviate from thresholds you've defined.

You can connect to your SAP account in the Data Center. This topic discusses the fields and menus that are specific to the SAP connector user interface. To add DataSets, set update schedules, and edit DataSet information, see [Adding a DataSet Using a Data Connector](/s/article/360042926274 "Adding a DataSet Using a Data Connector").

## Prerequisites

To connect to your SAP account and create a DataSet, you must have the following:

* Host URL
* Username
* Password

## Connecting to Your SAP Account

This section enumerates the options in the **Credentials** and **Details** panes in the SAP Connector page. The components of the other panes in this page, **Scheduling** and **Name & Describe Your DataSet**, are universal across most connector types and are discussed in greater length in [Adding a DataSet Using a Data Connector](/s/article/360042926274 "Adding a DataSet Using a Data Connector").
