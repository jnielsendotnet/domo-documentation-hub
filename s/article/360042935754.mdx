### Intro

Domo University is Domo's education hub. In Domo University, you will find valuable resources to help you make the most of your Domo experience. This article contains information about instructor-led training courses available to Domo users.

The courses listed below are offered as public virtual courses on a regular basis. They can also be delivered as private trainings, either virtually or in person, for just your team. To register for public courses, visit the [Instructor-Led Training](https://learndomo.domo.com/pages/25/instructor-led-training "Domo University Instructor-Led Training") <img src="/images/kb/ka0Vq000000DFSf-00N5w00000Ri7BU-0EMVq000007efOb.jpg" style={{width: 20, height: 20, display: 'inline', verticalAlign: 'start', margin: '0'}}/> page at Domo University. For more details on purchasing or scheduling training, please contact your Domo account team.

**Description**

Domo Hands-on is a practical, instructor-led training series designed to deepen your technical expertise across all major areas of the Domo platform. Through these interactive 60- and 90-minute sessions, you’ll gain the skills needed to build powerful analytics solutions with confidence. Whether you want to dive into Magic ETL, build dynamic dashboards, set up Personalized Data Permissions (PDP), or explore Jupyter Workspaces, this series provides hands-on experience to help you maximize your data’s impact though Domo’s powerful tools and capabilities.

**Who should take these courses?**

Domo users wanting practical experience with specific tools and features in Domo.

### Domo Immersion

For NEW and INTERMEDIATE Domo users wanting an immersive, comprehensive training experience, Domo University offers multi-day Domo Immersion training.

Domo Immersion is available in two formats:

* **Virtual immersion —** Public or Private Course - Six 4-hour sessions over two weeks (6 days)
* **In-person immersion at Domo HQ —** Public or Private Course - Seven 3-hour sessions (3.5 days)

**Who should take this course?**
  
New and Intermediate Domo users needing experience in data connection and transformation, content creation, and platform governance.

**Course Description**

In this course, our technical trainers will guide learners through the process of implementing a new Domo use case from start to finish. From uncovering business questions, to connecting, transforming, and visualizing data, users will gain hands-on experience with the core features of Domo learn how to apply governance and administration best practices along the way.

**Learning Objectives**

* Set and manage governance best practices for datasets, dashboards, users, and cards
* Assess and evaluate data connection options and general requirements
* Combine and transform multiple datasets to improve value and relevance for answering business requirements
* Create cards and dashboards to address business objectives
* Build an operational dashboard framework for multiple audiences
* Use available resources to answer questions and improve user capabilities
* Discuss how to apply lessons learned to live environment

**How to access this course?**

Domo Immersion may be included in your Support + Education bundle. To learn more about your bundle, contact your account team.
