## Intro

ZiftOne is a partner relationship management (PRM) platform that helps businesses manage and optimize partner channels. Use this connector to import partner activity events, user sign-ins, and system interaction data into Domo for analysis. Learn more about the [ZiftOne API](https://supplier.ziftone.com/login).

You connect to your ZiftOne account in the Data Center. This topic discusses the fields and menus that are specific to the ZiftOne connector user interface. General information for adding DataSets, setting update schedules, and editing DataSet information is discussed in [Adding a DataSet Using a Data Connector](/s/article/360042926274).

## Prerequisites

To connect to your ZiftOne account and create a DataSet, you must have the following:

* The Username used to log into your Unifyr platform.
* The Password used to log into your Unifyr platform.

## Connecting to Your ZiftOne Account

This section enumerates the options in the **Credentials** and **Details** panes in the ZiftOne Connector page. The components of the other panes in this page, **Scheduling** and **Name & Describe Your DataSet**, are universal across most connector types and are discussed in greater length in [Adding a DataSet Using a Data Connector](/s/article/360042926274 "Adding a DataSet Using a Data Connector").
