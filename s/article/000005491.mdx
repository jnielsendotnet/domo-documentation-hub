## Intro

Amazon Ads helps you find, attract, and engage millions of Amazon customers at every stage of their journey, where they share, read, listen, purchase, research, and download online. With your Amazon Ads data in Domo, you get an overview of how your advertising is performing. You can see how your content is performing across devices and make adjustments to improve product visibility and boost brand awareness.

You can connect to your Amazon Ads Advanced account in the Data Center. This topic discusses the fields and menus that are specific to the Amazon Ads Advanced connector user interface. To add DataSets, set update schedules, and edit DataSet information, see [Adding a DataSet Using a Data Connector](https://domo-support.domo.com/s/article/360042926274?language=en_US "Adding a DataSet Using a Data Connector").

## Prerequisites

To connect to your Amazon Ads Advanced account and create a DataSet, you must the following:

* Select a Marketplace Region
* Your Amazon login credentials.

## Connecting to Your Amazon Ads Advanced Account

This section enumerates the options in the **Credentials** and **Details** panes in the Amazon Ads Advanced Connector page. The components of the other panes in this page, **Scheduling** and **Name & Describe Your DataSet**, are universal across most connector types and are discussed in great detail in [Adding a DataSet Using a Data Connector](https://domo-support.domo.com/s/article/360042926274?language=en_US "Adding a DataSet Using a Data Connector").
