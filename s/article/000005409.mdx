---
title: "Convert Content from MS Office Legacy Plugins to MS Office Domo Add-ins"
---


### Intro

We have discontinued development of the Domo legacy plugins for Microsoft Office apps in favor of the [Domo add-ins for Microsoft Office 365 apps](https://domo-support.domo.com/s/topic/0TO5w000000ZkAlGAK/microsoft-office-addins?language=en_US).

To assist you in transitioning to the Domo add-ins, you can follow the steps below to convert content inserted with the legacy plugins so that it works with the Domo add-ins.

### Convert Content from Legacy Plugins

Follow these steps to convert your content to work with the Domo add-ins:

1. Install the Domo add-in(s) for your Microsoft Office apps. Learn how to
   [install the add-ins](/s/article/000005143).
2. In your Microsoft Office app, open the document that contains the content you want to convert.
3. Go to the **Domo** toolbar and select **Search** to open the Domo side rail. Learn more about the Domo toolbar and side rail in the [Domo add-ins user guide](https://domo-support.domo.com/s/article/000005143?language=en_US#AccesstheDomoSideRailinExcelPowerPointandWord).
4. In the Domo side rail, select
   <img src="/images/kb/ka0Vq0000000Z2b-00N5w00000Ri7BU-0EMVq000000T8dJ.jpg" style={{width: 20, height: 20, display: 'inline', verticalAlign: 'start', margin: '0'}}/> **Home** > **Settings**.
     
   <Frame><img alt="home settings.jpg" src="/images/kb/ka0Vq0000000Z2b-00N5w00000Ri7BU-0EMVq000000T9r7.jpg" style={{width: 225, height: 549}}/></Frame>
5. Under **Settings**, locate the **Convert from Legacy Plugins** area and select **Scan Now** to scan your document for content inserted from the legacy plugins.
     
   The document is scanned for legacy content and identified in the Domo side rail.
6. Check the **Select all** box or check individual boxes for each item you want to convert.

   <Warning>
     **Important:** After items are converted, you can no longer use the legacy plugins to modify the content. This action is not reversible. Consider taking a backup of this document before proceeding.

   </Warning>

   <Frame><img alt="Screenshot 2024-03-06 at 2.32.03 PM.png" src="/images/kb/ka0Vq0000000Z2b-00N5w00000Ri7BU-0EMVq000000T9xZ.jpg" style={{width: 224, height: 384}}/></Frame>
7. Use the **Convert Selected** option to convert your content.
     
   <Frame><img alt="convert selected.jpg" src="/images/kb/ka0Vq0000000Z2b-00N5w00000Ri7BU-0EMVq000000TAC5.jpg" style={{width: 224, height: 155}}/></Frame>
   The successfully converted content is listed in the **Identified** tab of the Settings.
     
   <Frame><img alt="Screenshot 2024-03-06 at 2.23.09 PM.png" src="/images/kb/ka0Vq0000000Z2b-00N5w00000Ri7BU-0EMVq000000T9eD.jpg" style={{width: 224, height: 239}}/></Frame>
The converted content is now available in the **Document** section of the Domo side rail. Learn to use the Domo add-ins in the [add-ins user guide](/s/article/000005143).
