title: "Update Payment Details for Domo Free"
---

import {InlineImage} from "/snippets/InlineImage.mdx";

If you need to update your payment information for Domo Free, such as in the event of a failed payment, follow this link to [Stripe <InlineImage src="/images/kb/ka0Vq00000001FR-00N5w00000Ri7BU-0EMVq0000000XDh.jpg" />](https://public.domo.com/payments/v1/subscriptions/portal) to manage your billing details.

Log in with your Stripe credentials, rather than with Domo credentials. Alternatively, you can access Stripe from within Domo by following the steps below.

<Frame><img alt="Screenshot 2023-12-05 at 6.14.25 AM.png" src="/images/kb/ka0Vq00000001FR-00N5w00000Ri7BU-0EMVq0000000XNN.jpg" style={{width: 900, height: 429}}/></Frame>

<Note>
  **Note:** If you are experiencing failed payments and updating your payment method is not resolving the issue, please contact your financial institution.
</Note>

To access the billing management portal from inside Domo, follow these steps:

1. Log into the Domo instance for which you are updating payment information.
2. In the usage bar at the top of the screen, select **Manage billing** to access your billing details in Stripe.
     
   <Frame><img alt="usage bar.jpg" src="/images/kb/ka0Vq00000001FR-00N5w00000Ri7BU-0EM5w000006wFoY.jpg" style={{width: 903, height: 556}}/></Frame>

3. In Stripe, make changes to your payment method, billing information, or other settings as needed.
     
   <Frame><img alt="manage domo sub.jpg" src="/images/kb/ka0Vq00000001FR-00N5w00000Ri7BU-0EM5w000006wH8e.jpg" style={{width: 901, height: 441}}/></Frame>
